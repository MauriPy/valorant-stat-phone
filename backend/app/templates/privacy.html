Privacy Policy

{{ app_name }} respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and your choices. This product is not affiliated with Riot Games, Inc.

1. Data we collect

  • Device data: device ID, firmware version, pairing code, device secret, last seen timestamp.
  • Account data (after opt-in): Riot ID (game name + tag), PUUID, OAuth tokens when using Riot Sign On.
  • Game statistics: recent match stats (KDA, map, agent) retrieved from Tracker Network and/or Riot API.
  • Technical logs: basic server logs (IP, request time) for security and debugging.

2. How we use data

  • Pair your physical device with your Riot account.
  • Fetch and display your latest match statistics on the OLED.
  • Operate, secure, and improve the service.

We do not sell your personal data.

3. Legal basis

Processing is based on your explicit consent when you link your Riot account and configure the device. You may withdraw consent by resetting the device.

4. Third parties

  • Riot Games — account verification (RSO) and official VALORANT APIs when enabled.
  • Tracker Network — player statistics when configured as data provider.
  • Hosting provider — where the backend is deployed (e.g. VPS, Railway).

Each third party has its own privacy policy and terms.

5. Retention

Device and account link data is kept while your device is active. OAuth tokens are stored to refresh access to your stats. You may request deletion by contacting us or resetting the device.

6. Security

Devices authenticate with a unique secret. Production deployments use HTTPS. No system is 100% secure; report issues responsibly via GitHub.

7. Your rights

Depending on your region (e.g. GDPR, CCPA), you may request access, correction, or deletion of your data. Contact us through the GitHub repository issues page.

8. Children

The service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children without parental consent.

9. Changes

This policy may be updated. The "Last updated" date at the bottom reflects the latest version.
